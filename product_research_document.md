Salton Business Solutions builds modern digital products using advanced technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), TypeScript, React.js, Next.js, Vue.js, and Tailwind CSS to deliver fast, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces. The company develops powerful backend systems using Node.js, Express.js, PHP, and Laravel, supported by secure and scalable database solutions such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. It also creates intelligent systems powered by AI, machine learning, automation, and data analytics to improve business efficiency and decision-making. For mobile development, Salton Business Solutions delivers cross-platform applications using React Native, Flutter, and Progressive Web Apps (PWA) to ensure seamless performance across Android, iOS, and web platforms. By integrating cloud computing services, RESTful APIs, cybersecurity best practices, and modern DevOps workflows, the company provides robust, scalable, and future-ready digital solutions that support continuous business growth and innovation.
